My Sunday Routine

A glimpse into my relaxing and productive Sundays

How I Spend Most Of My Sunday Mornings:

I start my Sunday laying "5 minutes extra" in bed and wake up 2 more hours later.

Next up I have a gigantic amount of 'Brunch' because that is probably going to be the only meal I have. (I will be too lazy to have dinner in a few hours.)

My Afternoons look like:

I start off with exploring one of my very new interests even further, ✨CODING✨

Next, one of my oldest hobbies get an appointment! ✨READING NOVELS✨

When the end of the afternoon is around the corner, i binge watch some of my favourite movies or shows. Just a little girl afterall!

Ending My Sunday With (*crying*):

As the sun starts to set, i go out for a nice and cozy evening walk to admire the pretty sky.

I sometimes write about my day to remind myself how grateful I am for this much needed break i got.

At last I put on my favourite songs depending on the mood i am to feel the sense of enjoyment.

I review any pending submissions or assignments and drift into sweet slumber right after getting over with it.
